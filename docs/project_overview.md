# Project Scope

**Project Title:** Digital Park Guide Training Platform for Sarawak's National Parks and Nature Reserves
**Industry Partner:** Sarawak Forestry Corporation (SFC)

---

## Introduction

Park guides are essential in delivering high-quality visitor experiences across Sarawak's national parks and nature reserves. Current training programs face physical constraints, lengthy durations, and inconsistent access to up-to-date conservation knowledge, limiting guide participation and skill development.

To address these challenges, SFC is developing a digital training platform accessible via mobile app and website. The platform provides flexible, interactive training modules covering conservation, biodiversity, eco-tourism, legislation, and safety, while enabling guides to track their progress and certifications efficiently.

The platform additionally incorporates:
- Cybersecurity measures to protect user data and ensure secure access
- An AI-based monitoring system using body-worn cameras to detect abnormal activities in real time, enhancing conservation enforcement and accountability
- Optional VR/AR simulations for immersive training experiences, allowing guides to practise practical skills in realistic park scenarios

---

## Problem Statements

**1. Limited Access to Training**
Current physical training programs are inflexible, requiring guides to take time off work or sacrifice income, which reduces enrollment and limits access to essential knowledge.

**2. Knowledge Gaps and Insufficient Monitoring**
Guides may lack continuous access to up-to-date conservation and eco-tourism practices, and there is no systematic way to monitor guided tours for compliance with park regulations, affecting service quality and conservation outcomes.

---

## Objectives

**Objective 1: Develop a Digital Training Platform**
- Create a mobile and web-based platform that provides modular, interactive training content covering conservation, biodiversity, eco-tourism, legislation, and safety. Include progress tracking, certification management, and notifications to support flexible learning.

**Objective 2: Ensure Cybersecurity and Data Protection**
- Implement secure login, encryption, and access control to protect guide profiles, training records, and assessment data, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations and maintaining trust in the platform.

**Objective 3: Implement AI-Based Monitoring for Conservation Enforcement**
- Integrate an AI-powered abnormal activity detection system using body-worn cameras to monitor guided tours in real time. Automatically trigger alerts for prohibited activities and securely store video evidence for review and reporting.

**Objective 4 (Optional): Enhance Training with VR/AR Simulations**
- Develop immersive VR/AR scenarios that replicate realistic park environments and guiding situations, improving practical skills, knowledge retention, and confidence while reducing logistical constraints of on-site training.

## Areas of Focus

### 1. Interactive Digital Training Platform

**Platform:** Mobile app and website

**Features:**
- Modular training content covering conservation, biodiversity, eco-tourism, legislation, and safety
- Multimedia resources: videos, images, interactive infographics, and quizzes
- Progress tracking and certification management

**Outcome:** Guides can learn flexibly, complete courses at their own pace, and stay updated with the latest park management and conservation practices.

**Basic Functionalities:**

*Admin role:*
- Set up training modules
- Keep track of park guide learning progress, qualifications, and certifications

*Park Guide role:*
- Sign up for related trainings
- Keep track of personal qualifications and certifications
- Receive notifications from Admin

---

### 2. Cybersecurity and Data Protection

**Implementation:**
- Secure login for all users
- Encryption for personal data, performance records, and assessments
- Access control for admins, trainers, and guides

**Outcome:** Protect sensitive information, ensuring compliance with data protection regulations and maintaining trust in the platform.

**Basic Features:**

*All platforms:*
- Secure access controls and encryption for protecting sensitive data
- Simple authentication methods to prevent unauthorised access

**Application Security Aspects:**
- Project planning and execution based on Secure Software Development Lifecycle (SSDLC)
- Application vulnerability assessment details, including tools to be used and scope of testing, defined in a testing plan
- Application vulnerability assessment results with verification of vulnerabilities
- Recommendations and implementation of recommendations to address vulnerabilities
- Evidence of application vulnerability reassessment to verify that vulnerabilities have been addressed successfully

---

### 3. IoT-Based Abnormal Activity Detection for Conservation Enforcement

**Implementation:**
- Equip park guides with IoT-enabled body-worn cameras during guided tours
- Real-time video streams are analysed using computer vision and AI models to detect abnormal or prohibited activities, including:
  - Plucking or damaging protected plants
  - Relocating, disturbing, or handling wildlife
  - Any actions violating park regulations
- Automatic real-time alerts to park authorities or rangers when abnormal activities are detected
- Secure video storage for review, reporting, and training purposes

**Outcome:**
- Proactive monitoring of conservation compliance during guided tours
- Enhanced protection of flora and fauna through early detection and rapid response
- Strengthened accountability and professionalism among park guides

**Basic Functionalities:**
- Real-time abnormal activity detection
- Automated alert system for park rangers and administrators
- Secure video recording, storage, and access for investigation and training purposes

---

**Outcome:**

**Basic Functionalities:**
